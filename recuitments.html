India Airport Services Recruitment 2018 for Ground Handling Staff Posts

India Airport Services Limited has published a notification for the recruitment of Ground Staff vacancies for its Northern Region, Delhi airport, Jaipur airport, Gaya airport, Varanasi airport, Pune airport, amd Mumbai airport on permanent basis. Interested and Eligible Unmarried Indian nationals (Male/ Female) can apply for the posts

No. of Posts : 5863

Closing date : 30-04-2018

Emoluments : During the period of training, the candidates will be given a stipendof Rs.10,000/- p.m.

Age Limit : Candidates age should be in between 18 and 35 years as on 25-03-201 8. Age relaxation is admissible 5 years in case of SC/ ST and 3 years in case of OBC candidates as per rules.

Educational Qualification : Candidates should possess 10+2 from a Government recognized Board/ University with relevant experience.

Selection Process :

Candidates who fulfill the above eligibility criteria for the post of Ground Handling Staff will be required to register themselves online and eligible candidates would be required to appear for a Medical examination with regard to Height and BMI at the Test Centre in the morning and those who would be declared eligible on the medical parameters would be required to appear for a Written Test. The Time and Venue will be intimated in due course

Application Fee : Applicants required to pay Rs. 600/- in the form of Demand Draft in favour of “India airport services", payable at Delhi

How to Apply : Eligible candidates can apply online through the website www.indiaairportservices.co.in

Note : Candidates who will disqualified in medical examination, those will refunded the application fee.

Important Dates

Last Date to Apply Online : 30-04-2018
